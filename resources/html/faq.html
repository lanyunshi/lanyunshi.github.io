Q:

What should I write if I would like to contact you by email?

A:

Please send me your CV, transcripts and a brief statement including what directions/tasks you would like to explore and what knowledge you have collected for them.

Q:

What is the characteristics of students that you would like to recuit?

A:

I am eager for students who are self-motivated, serious and diligent. Specifically, self-motivated indicates you know what you want to do and who you want to be. I may not have so much time to catch up with you frequently, but I do except that you can give me feedback initially. serious indicates you can have your private space, I keep respect to your personal instersts but I do think we should have the same goal and you should take the work that I assign to you seriously. diligent indicates that research is not always smooth, but you should be open to the research questions and do not give up easily.
